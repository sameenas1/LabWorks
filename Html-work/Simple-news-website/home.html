PROPOSAL FOR TIRUR-NILAMBUR METRO PROJECT

A proposal for a new metro line connecting Tirur and Nilambur in Malappuram district has been presented in the Kerala Assembly. The proposed Tirur-Nilambur metro aims to enhance connectivity in the Malappuram metropolitan area, with plans to construct the project in multiple phases covering a total length of 80 km.


HUMAN-ELEPHANT CONFLICT ESCALATES IN KERALA

The state has witnessed a concerning rise in human-elephant conflicts, resulting in multiple fatalities in recent months. Notably, in February 2025, a young man was killed in a wild elephant attack in Meppadi, Wayanad, and a 60-year-old man lost his life in Idukki district.


KERALA GOVERNMENT LAUNCHES 'GREEN KERALA' INITIATIVE TO PROMOTE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

The Kerala state government has unveiled the 'Green Kerala' initiative, aiming to promote sustainable development and environmental conservation across the state. This comprehensive program focuses on increasing forest cover, promoting renewable energy sources, and implementing waste management systems.
